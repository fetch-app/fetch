Fetch makes it easier than ever to find a dog. It is an easy to use application to browse available dogs from shelters, rescue organizations and breeders that uses a first of its kind standardized pre-approval form. Fetch is revolutionizing the process of buying or adopting a dog.

Fetch is addictive and fun.

It all started with the realization that there are many websites and apps out there that point us in the direction of dogs to adopt but there isn’t one site that brings it all together in an informative and engaging way that leads directly to finding a dog to take home.

The Fetch experience is designed to replicate a site that focuses on finding friends. After all, your dog will become your best friend. We interact in real life through online and in-person. Shouldn’t the dog adoption process do the same?
